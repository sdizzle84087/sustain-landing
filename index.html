Why We Built This

Born from Real Life Chaos

Last April, my 3-year-old daughter was diagnosed with leukemia. That same night — literally hours later — my wife went into labor and gave birth to our son on the couch of my daughter's hospital room.

Two weeks later, our daughter was back in the hospital with complications. A few weeks after that, our washing machine flooded our house from the second floor to the basement. It wasn't under warranty anymore. Insurance fought us on every dollar.

I was patching drywall at midnight between hospital visits and diaper changes, trying to figure out which items were still covered and which weren't. Six months later, our basement flooded again from a storm. Same insurance fights. Same scramble to document everything.

No family should have to dig through filing cabinets during the worst moments of their lives trying to find a warranty document or prove what they own.

— Jay, Founder of Sustain

That's when I decided to build Sustain. Not because I wanted to be a tech founder — I'm a data analyst, not a developer. But because the tools I needed simply didn't exist. So I built them, powered by AI, to help other families avoid the chaos mine went through.

My daughter has a 97% cure rate and is doing amazing. My son is healthy and growing. And now I'm channeling everything I learned into an app that protects what matters most — so when life throws you the unexpected, paperwork is the last thing on your mind.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sustain really free to use? ▼
Yes! You can track unlimited items, get product recall alerts, use barcode scanning, and set up warranty expiration reminders — all completely free, forever. Premium unlocks AI-powered features like photo identification, product page scanning, the claim assistant, and PDF export.
How does the recall checking work? ▼
Sustain automatically checks every item you add against the official U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) database. If a product you own has been recalled, you'll get an alert with details and instructions. Checks happen automatically in the background and are completely free.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and stored securely on Supabase (built on PostgreSQL with row-level security). We never sell your data, and we don't show ads. Your inventory is private to your account.
Can I use it for insurance claims? ▼
Yes — that's one of the core use cases. Premium users can generate a complete home inventory PDF with photos, serial numbers, purchase prices, and replacement values. This report gives insurance adjusters exactly what they need, which can save you thousands during a claim.
What languages are supported? ▼
Sustain is available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, and German. The AI features (chat assistant, warranty lookups, maintenance plans) also respond in your selected language. More languages coming soon.
Is it available on iPhone? ▼
Yes! Sustain is available on both iOS and Android. Download on the App Store or Google Play.
Who built this? ▼
Sustain was built by Jay, a data analyst and father in Salt Lake City. After his family experienced a cancer diagnosis, emergency birth, and two home floods in one year, he built the app he wished existed — to help families protect what matters most without the chaos of paperwork and lost receipts.
